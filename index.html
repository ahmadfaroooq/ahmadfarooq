Coin Bureau 10× LinkedIn Growth for Crypto's Biggest Media Brand
+

The Challenge

Coin Bureau had massive YouTube and newsletter audiences but a dormant LinkedIn presence. They needed a systematic approach to content distribution that would drive newsletter subscribers from a professional audience without alienating their crypto-native following.


The Approach

Built a LinkedIn content system repurposing their existing research into executive-friendly formats. Defined audience segments, posting cadence, and hook frameworks. Launched a 90-day testing sprint across content types and engagement tactics.

Results

10× LinkedIn following in 6 months
30% Of newsletter subs from LinkedIn

EdTech Founder 0 to Authority: Building a $300K Pipeline from LinkedIn
+

The Challenge

A Series A EdTech founder had product-market fit but zero personal brand presence. Inbound leads were stagnant and they relied entirely on outbound SDRs burning through budget. LinkedIn was an untapped channel with high-intent B2B buyers.


The Approach

Repositioned the founder from "CEO of EdTech startup" to "the future of corporate learning", a clear POV that attracted enterprise buyers. Built a 5-pillar content system, optimised the full LinkedIn profile, and established a 3× per week posting rhythm.

Results

12K Followers in 90 days (from 300)
$300K Pipeline attributed to LinkedIn
3 Inbound partnership deals in 90 days
Zedsource Complete Brand Identity for a Digital-First Staffing Company
+

The Challenge

Zedsource launched in a commoditised staffing market with no visual identity, no positioning, and no brand system. They were invisible. Every competitor looked identical: generic blue logos and corporate stock photography.


The Approach

Led a brand strategy sprint to define the positioning: "digital-first, founder-focused staffing." Designed a complete visual identity: logo, colour system, typography, and digital asset library that stood in sharp contrast to category conventions.

Results

100% Brand built from zero in 3 weeks
60% Reduction in CAC post brand launch
5× Profile visits after LinkedIn rebrand
