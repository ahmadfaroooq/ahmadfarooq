ZSourced — Complete Brand Identity

Brand Strategy · Visual Identity · Digital System

Client Brief

ZSourced launched in a commoditised staffing market with no visual identity, no positioning, and no brand system. Every competitor looked identical — generic blue logos and corporate stock photography.

Design Rationale

We leant into deep navy and electric blue to signal trust and competence, while an oversized wordmark and bold typographic lockup created instant differentiation. Every asset was built for LinkedIn-first distribution.

  • Full logo system (primary, secondary, icon mark)
  • Brand guidelines document (32 pages)
  • LinkedIn banner + profile visual kit
  • Business card and email signature
  • Social media template library (12 templates)

EdTech Founder — Investor Pitch Deck

Presentation Design · Investor Materials

Client Brief

A Series A EdTech startup needed a pitch deck that communicated their product vision and traction clearly to institutional investors — without looking like every other education technology deck.

Design Rationale

Used a deep purple and lime contrast system to signal innovation without losing credibility. Structured the narrative arc around the problem size first, making the investment case impossible to ignore by slide 5.

Ahmad Farooq — Personal Brand System

Web Design · Visual Identity · Content System

Brief

Built a complete personal brand system — from brand positioning and design language through to this website and LinkedIn visual identity. Neo-brutalism as deliberate positioning: systems-thinking made visible.

Design Rationale

Neo-brutalism was chosen intentionally — heavy borders, neon accents, and structured asymmetry communicate directness, expertise, and confidence. The warm cream base prevents it from feeling harsh while maintaining authority.
