Case Studies

Real growth. Real numbers.

  • Coin Bureau

    Content-Led Growth Driving 40% of Total Revenue

    Challenge

    Coin Bureau had massive content but no systematic distribution strategy. Traffic came in bursts after YouTube uploads then flatlined.

    Approach

    Built a cross-platform content distribution engine that turned each YouTube video into 12+ touchpoints across LinkedIn, email, and SEO articles. Implemented a newsletter flywheel that converted viewers into subscribers.

    Results

    • 40% of revenue from content channel
    • 2.1M+ organic impressions/month
    • 8% email-to-customer conversion rate
  • EdTech Founder

    Built a $2M ARR Acquisition Funnel From Scratch

    Challenge

    The founder relied 100% on paid ads with rising CAC and zero organic moat. Growth was fragile and expensive.

    Approach

    Audited the full funnel and identified the LinkedIn organic channel as massively underutilised. Built a 90-day content-to-demo framework that warmed leads before any sales conversation.

    Results

    • $2M ARR funnel built
    • CAC reduced by 45%
    • Demo-to-close rate increased to 38%
  • ZSourced

    60% CAC Reduction Through Demand-Gen Led Growth

    Challenge

    ZSourced was spending aggressively on outbound with diminishing returns. Brand recognition was zero. Every deal started cold.

    Approach

    Shifted strategy from outbound-first to demand-gen led — built brand awareness assets, a LinkedIn presence, and a content programme that made inbound inevitable.

    Results

    • 60% CAC reduction
    • 5× inbound lead volume
    • 90-day payback period
