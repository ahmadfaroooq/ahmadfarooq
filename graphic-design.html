Case Studies

Design that does more than look good.

  • ZSourced

    Complete Brand Identity Built in 3 Weeks

    Challenge

    ZSourced had no visual identity whatsoever. No logo, no colours, no fonts — nothing. And they needed to launch in 3 weeks. The brief was to build something distinctive and immediately professional that didn't look like another generic staffing company.

    Approach

    Ran a rapid brand strategy sprint followed by an intensive design phase. Designed the full logo system, colour palette, typography system, and core digital assets concurrently. Prioritised LinkedIn and web-first formats.

    Results

    • Complete brand identity delivered in 3 weeks
    • Immediately recognisable in their market
    • 5× increase in LinkedIn profile visits post rebrand
  • Coin Bureau

    Visual System for YouTube, LinkedIn, and Newsletter

    Challenge

    Coin Bureau's visual output was inconsistent — different styles across YouTube thumbnails, LinkedIn posts, and their newsletter. This made the brand feel smaller and less premium than it actually was.

    Approach

    Audited all existing visual touchpoints and built a unified design system with shared components, consistent colour application, and typography standards. Created a Figma library for the team to use independently.

    Results

    • Unified brand across 3 channels
    • 20-template Figma library delivered
    • Design turnaround time reduced by 70%
  • EdTech Founder

    Pitch Deck That Secured Series A Funding

    Challenge

    The EdTech founder had a strong business story but a presentation that undermined their credibility. The deck was a 40-slide PowerPoint with inconsistent formatting, stock photography, and no visual hierarchy.

    Approach

    Rebuilt the deck from scratch — restructured the narrative, designed every slide, and built a visual system that positioned the company as premium and sophisticated. Reduced from 40 to 22 slides without losing content.

    Results

    • Series A secured
    • Investor feedback: "Best deck we saw this round"
    • Deck design repurposed for product pitches and partnership decks
Process

How we build your visual identity.

  1. 01

    Brief

    Deep discovery session to understand your brand, audience, market positioning, and the feeling you want to create.

  2. 02

    Concept

    2-3 initial direction concepts with rationale. Explore different visual approaches before committing.

  3. 03

    Design

    Build out the chosen direction into a full system — logo, colours, typography, and core applications.

  4. 04

    Refine

    2 rounds of revisions to perfect every detail. The difference between good and exceptional is in the details.

  5. 05

    Deliver

    Final files in all formats (SVG, PNG, PDF). Brand guidelines document. Figma source files.

Ideal Client

Is this the right fit?

Good fit

  • Startups and founders launching or rebranding who want a distinctive visual identity
  • Personal brands needing a cohesive look across web and LinkedIn

Not a fit

  • Businesses wanting logo-and-done with no system thinking
  • Companies looking for the cheapest option (cheap design costs more long-term)
  • Those who want to redesign every 6 months chasing trends
  • Clients who design by committee with no single decision maker
