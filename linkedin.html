Ideal Client

Is this the right fit?

Good fit

  • Founders, executives, and CMOs who understand that their personal brand IS the company brand
  • Professionals with genuine expertise worth sharing
  • Companies where the founder's credibility directly impacts sales
  • People who can commit to 3 posts per week minimum

Not a fit

  • People who want LinkedIn managed 100% without their involvement (your voice, not mine)
  • Executives at large corporations with PR departments gatekeeping content
  • People who only want vanity metrics — followers with no business outcome
  • Those who aren't willing to share genuine opinions and perspectives
